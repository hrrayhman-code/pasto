We make five things. We make them properly. No frozen sauces, no shortcuts, nothing from a jar.

"In Italy, nonna doesn't open a jar. She opens her pantry — flour, eggs, tomatoes, garlic, cheese — and she makes the meal. That's all we do."

Pasto started in a home kitchen in Karachi with a simple goal: serving fresh, flavorful pasta made with quality ingredients. Every order is prepared fresh, our sauces are cooked with care, and meals are packed hot so they arrive warm and ready to enjoy.

We're small. We're new. We're cooking out of a home kitchen for now. But we think you can taste the difference, and we think you'll keep coming back.
